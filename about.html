We Believe

In Good.

Today ‘good’ isn’t just a nice-to-have creative pursuit. Not just a cherry on top of a vegan cake. It’s a business necessity.

As members of the Silicon Valley wilderness, we’ve always stood behind brave brands. Those that tap into creativity and collaboration to shape our world. Because everything else is just more noise.

We love projects that challenge us, pick clients that push us. And work with brands that inspire us. Is it the simplest road to take? Hell no. But the view is always good.

We call ourselves Nika, derived from the Persian word for ‘good’. Because our universe is centered around it. Good for our partners, their believers and our creatives.

BEHIND NIKA

Ali Tassavor

Ayman Eshaghpour

Nika is run by two partners, Ali Tassavor and Ayman Eshaghpour.

They combined their experiences from the tech and creative worlds, to create the new yin and yang of today’s needs

Prior to Nika the two worked for some of the biggest names in the industry, consistently producing award winning work. Ali’s technology background led him to groundbreaking companies like NASA and Disney. Ayman’s experience across 4 continents and legendary creative agencies like TBWA\Worldwide shaped his unique global creative perspective.

The two then pooled in their talents and passion to build Nika, the award winning creative boutique.
